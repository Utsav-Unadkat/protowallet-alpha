Features

ProtoWallet is jam-packed with features, and continuously keeps on evolving with new features.

  • 100% Free, Offline Only, Privacy First!
  • Monitor and Track Unlimited Expenses / Transactions.
  • Full-featured Budgeting Capability.
  • First-class - Realtime Analytics.
  • Import/Export to Excel and other formats.
  • No limit on number of Categories, Labels, Currencies that can be created.
  • Bulk editing, importing and migration support.
